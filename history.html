History & Legacy of Mercedes-Benz

Mercedes-Benz has been a pioneer in the automotive industry for over a century, leading innovation, luxury, and performance.

1886 - The First Automobile

Karl Benz created the first gasoline-powered automobile, the **Benz Patent Motorwagen**, marking the birth of the modern car.

1926 - Mercedes-Benz is Born

In 1926, **Mercedes-Benz** was officially formed after the merger of Benz & Cie and Daimler-Motoren-Gesellschaft.

1954 - The Iconic 300SL

The **300SL Gullwing** was introduced, becoming one of the most iconic sports cars in history.

1995 - Safety Innovations

Mercedes-Benz introduced **electronic stability control (ESC)**, a revolutionary technology that enhanced vehicle safety.

2022 - The Electric Revolution

The **EQS**, Mercedes' first fully electric luxury sedan, was launched, leading the way into sustainable mobility.
